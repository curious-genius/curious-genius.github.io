Curriculum Overview

Curious Genius runs 90-minute, project-based workshops that build computer science and digital literacy skills from Kindergarten to Grade 12.

  • Built on CSTA, Alberta CTS, Code.org, and ISTE standards.
  • Focus on algorithms, programming, data, networks, digital citizenship, and design thinking.
  • Hands-on projects with small groups (6–8 learners) and real-world artifacts.
