World-Class STEM Roadmap

Don't just play games—build them. Our university-ready curriculum scales with your child's ambition, from basic logic to professional Artificial Intelligence.

The Foundation

We use Icon-Based Coding (no reading required) to turn playtime into logical thinking sessions.

  • Sequencing: Solving puzzles by ordering commands correctly.
  • Pattern Recognition: Using loops to automate repetitive tasks.
  • Animation & Sound: Building their first interactive digital stories.
  • Hardware Basics: Identifying computer parts and safe device handling.

Featured Courses:

Tynker Junior, Space Cadet, All About Computers I

Outcome: Students develop the "Algorithm Mindset" before they even reach the 3rd Grade.

The Builders

Students transition to Visual Block Coding, building real games and modding their favorite worlds.

  • Game Design: Using variables, scoring, and multi-level logic.
  • Minecraft Modding: Manipulating game environments through custom code.
  • Robotics: Controlling hardware like micro:bit and LEGO WeDo.
  • Digital Citizenship: Mastering online safety and strong password security.

Featured Courses:

Programming 101, Minecraft Modding, micro:bit 101

Outcome: From passive consumers to active creators with real engineering logic.

The Innovators

The bridge to professional tech. Students learn Python, JavaScript, and AI.

  • Real-World Code: Writing text-based Python and Web Development (HTML/CSS).
  • AI & AR: Building apps that use Face Detection and Augmented Reality.
  • Physics Engines: Coding realistic gravity and collisions into platformer games.
  • Data Analysis: Collecting and visualizing data to make predictions.

Featured Courses:

Python Fundamentals, AI 101, JavaScript Web Dev

Outcome: Career-ready skills that look impressive on university applications.
