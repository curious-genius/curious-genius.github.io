FAQ

Does my child need experience?

No. This Journey is beginner friendly and instructor-led. We start from fundamentals and build step-by-step.

Is this real coding or only Roblox building?

Real coding. Students learn Lua scripting (events, variables, loops, conditionals) while building games in Roblox Studio.

What does my child need to bring?

Nothing special. We provide all required laptops and learning tools. Students only need to bring a water bottle and their curiosity to learn.

How do parents track progress?

Parents receive milestone updates, project screenshots/videos, and a final published game link.
